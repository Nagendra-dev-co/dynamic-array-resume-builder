About the creators
Our mission is to provide a free, open-source resume-building platform that empowers individuals from all backgrounds to craft compelling resumes without financial barriers. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to present their skills and experiences effectively. By leveraging open-source technology, we foster collaboration and innovation, allowing users to customize their resumes while contributing to a community-driven project. Our goal is to make resume creation accessible, flexible, and user-friendly, helping job seekers navigate their career paths with confidence. You can clone this project here.
